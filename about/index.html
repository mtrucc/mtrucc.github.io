About

I’m a passionate developer with a focus on frontend engineering and embedded development. I specialize in using React and Vue to create efficient and user-friendly interfaces, have extensive experience in developing desktop applications with Electron, and possess strong backend development skills with Node.js and Golang. In the field of embedded systems, I have also accumulated substantial hands-on experience, bridging hardware and software seamlessly.

Life

In both learning and work, I take pride in documenting every step of my technical exploration, capturing insights and lessons along the way.
